Scam Prevention

Taxi Scam: Never believe drivers who say your hotel is "closed or burned down". Demand to be taken to your booked address.
Fake Guides: Only hire government-approved (Incredible India) guides. Never hand over your passport.
ATMs: Use ATMs located inside actual bank branches with security guards to avoid card skimmers.

Solo Tourist Safety

Share your live location with family back home daily.
Do not walk alone in unlit alleys after 9 PM.
Use pre-booked cabs (Uber/Ola/BluSmart) rather than street autos at night.

Women Safety Protocol

Dial 1091 or 181 for immediate Women's Helpline assistance.
Women-only coaches are available in Metro trains (Usually the first coach).
Avoid responding to overly intrusive personal questions from strangers.
