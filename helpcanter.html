Account, security & community

The steps for the most common situations, across every Dozera and Pulse product.

Forgot password

Go to your product's sign-in page → "Forgot password" → enter the email on your account → open the reset link from your inbox (check spam) → set a new password. Links expire after a short time, so request a fresh one if it doesn't work.

Hacked or compromised account

Reset your password immediately from a trusted device. Sign out of all sessions in account settings if available. Then submit a support ticket below marked Urgent with your product name and the email tied to the account — do not share your password or OTP with anyone, including support.

Community guidelines

Be respectful across all Dozera, Oriontec, and Pulse platforms. No harassment, spam, impersonation, or sharing of others' private data. Educational and exam-related products (RNWD, Result Portal) require honest, individual use only. Violations may lead to suspension.

Account recovery without email access

If you no longer have access to your recovery email, submit a ticket with your full name, the product, and any order/registration details you remember. Our team verifies ownership manually — this can take longer than a standard reset.

Data & privacy requests

To request a copy or deletion of your data from any Dozera/Pulse product, submit a ticket with "Privacy Request" as the issue type. Never include passwords, OTPs, or financial details in any form field — see the notice on the support form.

Fastest way to get unblocked

For time-sensitive issues — exam access, urgent account lockouts — message Instagram support @oriontec.inc directly, or submit a ticket with priority set to Urgent. We triage urgent tickets first.
