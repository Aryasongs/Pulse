Unified Global Policies

The following 50 primary directives constitute the Unified Global Policy of the Dozera Group. These rules are non-negotiable and apply strictly to every user, enterprise client, and developer.

Respect and Safety

  1. Respect Everyone: We mandate a culture of mutual respect. Users must interact with courtesy across all Dozera platforms, from code collaboration on PS Code to communications on Mailbee.
  2. No Harassment: Sustained, targeted harassment, cyberbullying, or unwanted advances are strictly prohibited. This includes mobilizing others to target individuals.
  3. No Hate Speech: Content that promotes violence, attacks, or degrades individuals based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, caste, sexual orientation, sex, gender identity, or disability is permanently banned.
  4. No Threats: Any explicit or implicit threat of physical violence, financial ruin, or doxxing against an individual or group will lead to immediate account suspension and referral to law enforcement.
  5. No Terrorism Promotion: The Dozera Group has zero tolerance for terrorist organizations or violent extremist groups. Promotion, recruitment, or funding of such entities is an immediate legal violation.
  6. No Violence: Content that glorifies real-world violence, bodily harm, or self-harm is restricted across our ecosystem.
  7. No Child Exploitation: Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) is unequivocally forbidden. Our systems actively scan for such content. Violators are instantly reported to international authorities including NCMEC.
  8. User Safety: All Dozera products are designed with user safety at the forefront. We reserve the right to deploy automated systems to detect and intercept unsafe interactions.
  9. Child Safety: Minors operating on our platforms are protected by strict privacy defaults. Any attempt by adults to groom, exploit, or inappropriately contact minors will result in permanent hardware bans.

Prohibited and Illegal Activities

  1. No Illegal Activities: You may not use any Dozera product to plan, facilitate, or execute any action defined as a crime under international or local law.
  2. No Drug Sales: The facilitation, advertisement, or sale of illegal narcotics or unprescribed controlled substances is strictly prohibited on our networks.
  3. No Weapons Trading: Using platforms like OrioBuzz or CereusPay to facilitate the sale or exchange of firearms, explosives, or illegal weaponry is strictly forbidden.
  4. No Human Trafficking: Absolute prohibition on all forms of human exploitation, trafficking, and forced labor.
  5. No Financial Fraud: Engaging in ponzi schemes, advance-fee fraud, or deceptive financial practices via Jet2 Banking or CereusPay will trigger immediate asset freezing and legal action.
  6. No Identity Theft: Impersonating another person, brand, or entity to deceive others or harvest sensitive information is a critical violation.

Platform Security and Integrity

  1. No Account Selling: Dozera accounts are strictly non-transferable. Buying, selling, or trading accounts is prohibited and will result in the termination of the account.
  2. No Spam: Unsolicited mass messaging, automated bot replies, or deceptive self-promotion is banned.
  3. No Malware: Uploading, hosting, or distributing malicious payloads, trojans, or spyware on Nexor Cloud or PS Code is illegal.
  4. No Viruses: Dissemination of viral code intended to damage, disrupt, or gain unauthorized access to our infrastructure is strictly prohibited.
  5. No Ransomware: Utilizing Dozera infrastructure to host or distribute ransomware control servers will lead to immediate cooperation with global cybercrime units.
  6. No Phishing: Creating deceptive websites, forms, or emails via Mailbee to harvest user credentials will result in immediate domain blacklisting.
  7. No Reverse Engineering: Users may not decompile, reverse engineer, or attempt to extract the source code of any closed-source Dozera software or AI model.
  8. No Security Bypass: Attempting to circumvent 2FA, DRM, or encryption protocols within DSO or KALKI Security will result in a permanent ban.
  9. No Credential Theft: Engaging in brute-force attacks, credential stuffing, or session hijacking is strictly prohibited.
  10. No Unauthorized Scraping: Automated harvesting of user data, AI responses, or platform content without explicit API authorization is a violation of our Fair Usage Policy.
  11. No Fake Accounts: Creating inauthentic profiles for the purpose of platform manipulation, engagement farming, or evading bans is strictly forbidden.
  12. No Ban Evasion: Utilizing VPNs, alt-accounts, or hardware spoofing to bypass a previous suspension will result in IP-level and device-level restrictions.

Intellectual Property, Privacy & AI

  1. No Copyright Violations: We strictly adhere to the DMCA. Uploading infringing material to Nexor Cloud or Pulse Music will result in content removal and account strikes.
  2. No Trademark Abuse: Unauthorized use of Dozera Group trademarks or the trademarks of third parties to confuse or deceive consumers is prohibited.
  3. No Deepfake Abuse: The generation or distribution of non-consensual synthetic media (deepfakes) intended to harass, defame, or create pornographic material is permanently banned.
  4. AI Responsible Usage: Generative models must be used ethically. Users may not intentionally generate harmful, biased, or illegal content.
  5. Data Privacy: Respect the privacy of others. Doxxing—publishing private, sensitive information (such as addresses or phone numbers) without consent—is strictly forbidden.

Accountability, Moderation, and Business Ethics

  1. Platform Integrity: We actively monitor for and disrupt botnets, click-farms, and coordinated inauthentic behavior to maintain the integrity of platforms like OrioBuzz and Nova Search.
  2. Fair Usage Policy: API access, cloud storage, and compute resources must be utilized within acceptable, human-scale limits to ensure service stability for all users.
  3. Content Moderation: Dozera utilizes a hybrid approach of advanced AI filtering and human review to enforce these guidelines impartially.
  4. Appeals Process: Users have the right to appeal moderation decisions within 14 days via the Dozera Support Portal.
  5. Investigation Process: Complex cases involving enterprise data or severe abuse are subject to detailed internal investigations, during which accounts may be temporarily frozen.
  6. Temporary Restrictions: Minor or first-time offenses may result in temporary read-only modes or feature limitations.
  7. Permanent Suspension: Severe violations, such as terrorism, CSAM, or ransomware distribution, carry an immediate and permanent suspension without the right to appeal.
  8. Account Recovery: Restoring access to a compromised account requires strict identity verification procedures.
  9. Device Restrictions: Devices used to repeatedly violate these terms may be permanently blocked from accessing the Dozera ecosystem via hardware signature bans. Hardware compatibility rules also apply here; diagnostic values used for account recovery or investigations precisely distinguish between baseline models (e.g., standard iPhone 14) and Pro variants to ensure accurate telemetry.
  10. IP Restrictions: Malicious IP addresses and autonomous system numbers (ASNs) will be blacklisted across our entire cloud edge.
  11. Government Requests: We comply with legally binding, valid law enforcement requests and subpoenas, prioritizing user privacy where legally permissible.
  12. Emergency Disclosure: In situations involving imminent threat to life or serious physical injury, Dozera Group may voluntarily disclose data to emergency services.
  13. Transparency Reports: We commit to publishing bi-annual transparency reports detailing government requests, moderation actions, and network integrity operations.
  14. Reporting Abuse: All users are required to utilize in-app reporting tools to flag violations of these guidelines.
  15. Trusted Reporter Program: Verified NGOs, copyright holders, and cybersecurity experts may apply for expedited reporting channels.
  16. Business Accounts: Corporate entities must ensure all employees acting under their Dozera organizational account comply with these rules. Entities undergoing corporate acquisitions or mergers (e.g., acquiring independent record labels like World Entertainment) must update their Dozera corporate registry and compliance documentation within 30 days of the finalized agreement.
  17. Developer Accounts: Developers building on Dozera APIs must submit to periodic security audits and ensure their applications do not facilitate guideline violations.
  18. API Usage Rules: Scraping our APIs, bypassing rate limits, or utilizing unauthorized endpoints will result in immediate token revocation.

Product-Specific Guidelines

While the Unified Global Policies apply to all products, the following individual services enforce additional, specific rules tailored to their operational environments.

PS Code

  • Safe Coding: All repositories must comply with international security standards.
  • No Malicious Code: Hosting spyware, ransomware, keyloggers, or botnet command-and-control infrastructure is strictly prohibited.
  • No Exploit Sharing: Zero-day exploits and proof-of-concept attacks may only be shared under strict responsible vulnerability disclosure protocols for defensive purposes.
  • No Stolen Repositories: Plagiarizing or hosting stolen proprietary code will result in immediate DMCA takedowns.
  • Ethics & Collaboration: Ensure secure package publishing, open-source ethics, and secure collaboration. No Crypto Miners: Covert cryptocurrency mining scripts are banned.

PX

  • Project Collaboration & Workspace Security: Administrators must manage organization permissions responsibly. When inviting external collaborators (for example, inviting creative team members like Gurinder, Jatinder, or Anmol to a shared enterprise workspace), the primary account holder is strictly liable for their compliance.
  • Enterprise Integrity: File sharing must adhere to corporate data loss prevention (DLP) rules. Audit logs are permanently maintained to ensure insider abuse prevention and enterprise compliance.

PA Code

  • AI-Generated Code Responsibility: Developers are fully responsible for the security and testing of code generated by PA Code.
  • Secure Prompts: Attempting to prompt PA Code to write malware is prohibited. Continuous code review and licensing compliance for generated content ownership are mandatory.

DSO (Dozera System Operating)

  • Operating System Integrity: Modifying core system files to bypass telemetry or security protocols is forbidden.
  • Security Standards: Strict rules apply to system modification, driver security, boot security, and kernel protection. Secure updates must not be artificially blocked or tampered with.

Nova Search

  • Search Integrity: Engaging in search manipulation, ranking abuse, or deploying massive spam indexing networks will result in complete domain de-indexing.
  • Safe Search: Webmasters must correctly tag explicit content to ensure compliance with our Safe Search algorithms.

Astra AI, NEXOR AI & NEROX AI

  • Responsible AI: Interacting with Dozera LLMs and image models requires strict adherence to safety protocols.
  • Security Prohibitions: No jailbreak attempts, no prompt injection abuse, no model extraction attacks, and no harmful automation. AI transparency and AI safety are our primary operational metrics.

KALKI Security

  • Authorized Operations Only: Tools provided by KALKI are for defensive cybersecurity and authorized scanning only.
  • No Offensive Attacks: Unauthorized penetration testing against third-party servers is a federal crime and strictly prohibited.
  • Ethics: Adhere to responsible disclosure, malware research ethics, enterprise endpoint protection standards, and strict digital evidence handling procedures.

Nexor Cloud

  • Storage Integrity: Storage abuse, illegal file hosting, and distribution of pirated software will trigger automated account purges.
  • Data Management: Users must adhere to sharing permissions, storage quotas, and backup policies while maintaining proper encryption standards.

Mailbee

  • Anti-Spam & Authentication: Enterprise domains must implement strict email authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) to maintain domain reputation.
  • Security: Strict bulk sender rules apply. Phishing prevention protocols actively scan for and neutralize unsafe attachments and malicious links.

Pulse Music

  • Copyright & Licensing: Artists and independent labels (such as Sharan Records) must ensure absolute ownership or correct licensing of master recordings before uploading.
  • Metadata Integrity: When scheduling EP releases (such as an *American Love* EP) or rescheduling track launches (e.g., updating a release date to April 18 for tracks like *Midnight Tree*), metadata must be exact. Song titles must be spelled correctly in official romanization (e.g., utilizing standard spellings like *Judaa* instead of incorrect variants) to ensure accurate content ID matching.
  • Abuse Prevention: Royalty abuse and fake streaming prevention algorithms actively monitor for bot networks. Violators will have royalties withheld.

TravelGuard India

  • Location Privacy & Safety: Our mapping and routing APIs prioritize user privacy and travel safety above all.
  • Transit Operations: Features tailored for transit operators and vehicle rental companies ensure secure routing. Whether managing fleets of premium SUVs (such as the Toyota Fortuner, including specialized trims like Legender or Leader) or navigating regional transit routes through specific hubs (like Banga in Shaheed Bhagat Singh Nagar), our systems mandate adherence to government advisory compliance and emergency information protocols.

CereusPay & Jet2 Banking

  • Financial Compliance: Strict adherence to KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) regulations is required.
  • Fraud Prevention: All accounts are subject to continuous transaction monitoring. We enforce strict policies regarding payment disputes, chargebacks, banking security, financial fraud, and identity verification.

LUSY Medical Care (LMDC)

  • Healthcare Standards: Whether dealing with human diagnostics or veterinary information, strict health record privacy must be maintained.
  • Pet Care: We advocate for pet owner responsibilities and responsible pet care education across the LMDC network.

Enforcement & Disciplinary Action

The Dozera Group Trust & Safety division, supported by KALKI Security automated enforcement systems, maintains a rigid matrix of disciplinary actions to address violations of these Community Guidelines.

Enforcement Tier Action Description Typical Violations
Warning A formal digital notice delivered via email and the Dozera unified dashboard, indicating a minor breach of policy. Accidental copyright infringement, minor fair usage violations, inappropriate language.
Feature Restriction Temporary suspension of specific capabilities (e.g., revoking API post access, blocking Nova Search indexing). Spamming, minor prompt injection attempts, API rate limit abuse.
Temporary Suspension Complete loss of ecosystem access ranging from 24 hours to 30 days based on severity. Targeted harassment, hate speech, repeated minor policy violations.
Permanent Suspension Irreversible account deletion, revocation of licenses, and loss of all digital assets stored on Nexor Cloud. Severe harassment, financial fraud (CereusPay), malware distribution (PS Code).
Device / Hardware Ban Permanent blacklisting of the user's DSO hardware signature, preventing the device from ever connecting to Dozera networks. Ban evasion, severe reverse engineering attempts, unauthorized system modifications.
Identity Restrictions Phone number and Email restriction, permanently blacklisting these identifiers from creating new accounts. Creation of automated bot networks, running phishing campaigns via Mailbee.
Legal Action Filing civil suits, cooperating with law enforcement, and providing digital forensics to international authorities. CSAM distribution, terrorism promotion, human trafficking, major corporate data breaches.

Identity Verification: In cases of suspicious activity, accounts will be locked under "Dozera System Check." Users must pass enhanced cryptographic identity verification to restore access.
