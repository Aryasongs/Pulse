2. Unified Global Policies

The following 50 pillars form the foundation of our unified policy, applicable across every surface of the Dozera Group ecosystem.

Respect and Safety

  1. Respect Everyone: Treat all users, developers, and Dozera staff with dignity.
  2. No Harassment: Stalking, bullying, and targeted harassment are strictly prohibited.
  3. No Hate Speech: We do not tolerate attacks based on race, ethnicity, national origin, caste, sexual orientation, gender, or religion.
  4. No Threats: Any credible threat of physical or financial harm will result in immediate termination and law enforcement escalation.
  5. No Terrorism Promotion: Promotion of terrorist organizations or violent extremism is permanently banned.
  6. No Violence: Glorification or incitement of real-world violence is forbidden.
  7. No Child Exploitation: Zero tolerance for CSAM. All instances are reported immediately to NCMEC and international authorities.
  8. User Safety & Child Safety: (Policies 31 & 32) Products are engineered with safety-by-design. Minors must be protected from inappropriate content, predatory behavior, and unauthorized data collection.

Illegal and Regulated Activities

  1. No Illegal Activities: Using our platforms to facilitate crime is prohibited.
  2. No Drug Sales: Prohibition on the sale, promotion, or facilitation of illegal narcotics.
  3. No Weapons Trading: Facilitating the sale of firearms, explosives, or illegal weaponry is forbidden.
  4. No Human Trafficking: Absolute prohibition on all forms of exploitation and trafficking.
  5. No Financial Fraud: Phishing, ponzi schemes, and advance-fee fraud are banned.
  6. No Identity Theft: Impersonating others for deceptive purposes is a severe violation.

Platform Integrity and Security

  1. No Account Selling: Transferring or selling Dozera accounts is invalid and prohibited.
  2. No Spam: Mass automated messaging and unsolicited bulk interactions are banned.
  3. No Malware / Viruses / Ransomware: (Policies 17-19) Distributing malicious software, trojans, or ransomware across our cloud, email, or code platforms is strictly forbidden.
  4. No Phishing: Harvesting credentials via deceptive sites hosted on our infrastructure is prohibited.
  5. No Reverse Engineering: Unauthorized decompilation of Dozera proprietary software is banned.
  6. No Security Bypass: Attempting to circumvent our authentication or DRM systems will result in a hardware ban.
  7. No Credential Theft / Unauthorized Scraping: (Policies 23-24) Automated data harvesting without explicit API authorization is prohibited.
  8. No Fake Accounts / Ban Evasion: (Policies 25-26) Creating alt-accounts to bypass a suspension will result in IP and device-level bans.
  9. Platform Integrity: (Policy 33) We actively monitor for and disrupt botnets, click-farms, and coordinated inauthentic behavior.

Intellectual Property and AI

  1. No Copyright Violations: DMCA and global copyright laws are strictly enforced.
  2. No Trademark Abuse: Unauthorized use of Dozera or third-party trademarks is banned.
  3. No Deepfake Abuse: Generating non-consensual synthetic media (deepfakes) to harass or defame is prohibited.
  4. AI Responsible Usage: (Policy 30) Generative AI tools must not be used to bypass safety filters or generate prohibited content.
  5. Data Privacy: (Policy 31) Users must not share PII (Personally Identifiable Information) of others (doxxing).

Accountability and Operations

(Covering Policies 34-50) We enforce a strict Fair Usage Policy. Our Content Moderation utilizes hybrid AI and human review. Users have the right to an Appeals Process following an Investigation Process. Penalties range from Temporary Restrictions to Permanent Suspensions, including Device Restrictions and IP Restrictions. We comply with valid Government Requests and Emergency Disclosures, detailed in our annual Transparency Reports. Users can utilize Reporting Abuse tools or join the Trusted Reporter Program. Business, Developer, and API Accounts are subject to extended SLA and ethical compliance audits.

3. Product-Specific Guidelines

PS Code

  • Safe Coding: All repositories must comply with international security standards.
  • Prohibited Code: No malicious source code, spyware, ransomware, keyloggers, crypto miners, or botnet infrastructure may be hosted.
  • Ethics & Security: No exploit sharing without context of defensive research. Stolen repositories will be deleted. Ensure secure package publishing.
  • Open Source Ethics: We enforce responsible vulnerability disclosure and secure collaboration.

PX

  • Workspace Security: Project collaboration must respect organizational permissions.
  • Enterprise Compliance: Audit logs are maintained to prevent insider abuse. Unauthorized file sharing of proprietary corporate data is prohibited.

PA Code

  • AI-Generated Code Responsibility: Users are responsible for testing and validating AI-generated code.
  • Compliance: Secure prompts must be used; attempts to bypass coding safety guardrails are banned. Code review and licensing compliance for generated content ownership are mandatory.

DSO (Dozera System Operating)

  • System Integrity: Unauthorized system modifications that compromise kernel protection or boot security are prohibited.
  • Driver Security: Only cryptographically signed and verified drivers may be deployed. Secure updates must not be bypassed.

Nova Search

  • Search Integrity: No search manipulation, SEO spam indexing, or ranking abuse.
  • Safe Search: Explicit content must respect Safe Search parameters. Deceptive cloaking is prohibited.

Astra AI / NEXOR AI / NEROX AI

  • Responsible AI: Strict prohibition on jailbreak attempts and prompt injection abuse.
  • Security: No model extraction, weight theft, or harmful automation. All interactions are subject to AI safety and transparency guidelines.

KALKI Security

  • Authorized Operations: Defensive cybersecurity and authorized scanning only.
  • Prohibitions: No offensive attacks or unauthorized penetration testing against unowned assets.
  • Ethics: Strict adherence to malware research ethics, enterprise endpoint protection standards, and digital evidence handling chains of custody.

Nexor Cloud

  • Storage Abuse: Illegal file hosting and pirated software distribution will result in account deletion.
  • Zero Tolerance: CSAM prohibition is absolute.
  • Management: Users must respect storage quotas, sharing permissions, backup policies, and use encryption responsibly.

Mailbee

  • Anti-Spam & Phishing: Phishing prevention and bulk sender rules are strictly enforced.
  • Authentication: Email authentication (DKIM, SPF, DMARC) is required for enterprise domains to maintain domain reputation. Safe attachments are mandatory.

Pulse Music

  • Rights Management: Strict enforcement of copyright, music licensing, and artist protection.
  • Integrity: Royalty abuse and fake streaming prevention (bot listening) are actively monitored and penalized.

TravelGuard India

  • Safety & Privacy: Location privacy is paramount. Information provided must aid travel safety and emergency information distribution.
  • Compliance: Users must adhere to local and national government advisory compliance.

CereusPay & Jet2 Banking

  • Financial Compliance: Mandatory KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) verification.
  • Security: Fraud prevention, transaction monitoring, identity verification, and strict rules regarding payment disputes and chargebacks.

LUSY Medical Care (LMDC)

  • Veterinary & Pet Care: Information shared must support responsible pet care and pet owner responsibilities.
  • Privacy: Strict health record privacy for both human and veterinary subjects.

4. LUSY ACT — Every Life Matters

Inspired by Lusy's legacy, Dozera Group has enshrined the protection, respect, and love for animals into our core corporate DNA.

  • Dogs are welcome across all Dozera Group and Oriontec properties where legally permitted.
  • Zero tolerance for animal cruelty on our platforms and in our physical spaces.
  • Report animal abuse immediately through our dedicated Trust & Safety channels.
  • We actively encourage rescue, adoption, and responsible ownership.
  • Dozera facilities commit to providing clean drinking water for animals whenever possible.
  • We promote kindness toward every animal.
  • Employees found abusing animals may face disciplinary action up to and including termination.
  • Users promoting or glorifying animal cruelty may be permanently banned without appeal.
  • Respect service animals at all times.
  • We use our platform reach to encourage education about responsible pet care.
"Every Dog Is Welcome. Every Life Matters. Kindness Is Mandatory."

5. Enforcement & Disciplinary Action

Our Trust and Safety teams, along with automatedKALKI Security systems, monitor the ecosystem. Violations of these guidelines will result in enforcement actions scaled to the severity of the offense.

Enforcement Action Description Typical Triggers
Warning Formal notice sent to the user via email and dashboard. Minor fair usage violations, accidental copyright infringement.
Feature Restriction Temporary loss of specific capabilities (e.g., API access, posting). Spamming, prompt injection attempts on AI, API rate limit abuse.
Temporary Suspension Complete loss of account access for 24 hours to 30 days. Harassment, hate speech, repeated minor violations.
Permanent Suspension Irreversible deletion of account and loss of digital assets. Severe harassment, financial fraud, malware distribution.
Device / Hardware Ban DSO/Hardware signature is permanently blocked from Dozera networks. Ban evasion, severe hacking/reverse engineering attempts.
Phone/Email Restriction Identifiers are blacklisted across the Dozera ecosystem. Creation of bot networks, phishing campaigns.
Legal Action & Law Enforcement Filing civil suits or referring data to international authorities. CSAM, terrorism, human trafficking, major data breaches.

Identity Verification & Account Recovery: If your account is flagged for suspicious activity, you may be required to complete enhanced identity verification to regain access. All appeals must be routed through the Dozera Support Portal.
