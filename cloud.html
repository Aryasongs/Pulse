About Us

Nexor Cloud is a personal secure workspace built for people who want their files, notes, contacts, and passwords organized in one private place.

We built Nexor around a simple idea: your everyday digital life — documents, photos, reminders, saved logins — shouldn't be scattered across a dozen different apps. It should live in one fast, well-designed home that respects your time and your privacy.

Nexor is actively developed, and new features ship regularly based on what people actually need day to day.

Questions or feedback? Reach out through your account email and we'll get back to you.

Privacy Policy

What we store

Your files, notes, contacts, saved passwords, and reminders are stored in your account, tied to your login email. Files live in Realtime Database; other data lives in Firestore — both provided by Firebase (Google Cloud).

Who can see your data

Only you, by default. If you generate a share link for a file, anyone with that link can view it until you revoke sharing. If you connect with another user, only the contents of your shared folder become visible to them — nothing else in your account.

Account data

We store your name and email to identify your account and to let other users find you when sending a connection request. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Your control

You can delete individual files, notes, contacts, or passwords at any time. Deleted files pass through Trash before permanent removal (30 or 90 days depending on your plan). You can remove a connection at any time, which also removes access to its shared folder.

Changes to this policy

As Nexor evolves, this policy may be updated to reflect new features. Material changes will be reflected here.
