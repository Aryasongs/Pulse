How can we help?

Find point-to-point answers for every feature on Sparks — posting, profiles, privacy, lists, and more.

Getting Started

Creating an account and signing in.

How do I create a Sparks account?
Open the Sign up tab on the welcome screen, enter your name, email, handle, and password, then submit. You'll land straight in your feed once your account is created.
How do I log in?
Use the Log in tab and enter the email and password you signed up with. Stay on the app and you'll remain signed in.
I forgot my password — what now?
Tap Forgot password on the login screen and enter your account email. You'll receive a reset link to set a new password.
How do I sign out?
Open your profile menu from the sidebar (or the "More" menu on mobile) and tap Sign out.

Posting a Pulse

Composing, attaching media, and character limits.

How do I post a Pulse?
Tap New Pulse in the sidebar, or use the compose box at the top of your feed. Write your text, attach media if you'd like, then tap Post.
What's the character limit?
Regular accounts can write up to 200 characters per Pulse. Verified accounts get a higher limit of 780 characters.
Can I attach images or video?
Yes — tap the image icon to attach photos (multiple images form a grid), or the video icon to attach a short clip (max 15 seconds). You can remove an attachment with the × button before posting.
Can I edit a photo before posting it?
Yes — tap the edit icon on an attached image to open the built-in editor. You can rotate, flip, adjust brightness/contrast/saturation, and apply filters (black & white, sepia, vivid, cool, warm) before saving.
Why is an image blurred with a warning?
That's the sensitive-content overlay. Images flagged as potentially adult/sensitive are automatically blurred — tap through the overlay to reveal them. You can toggle this blur on or off in Settings.
What does the small "AI" label mean?
Pulses containing AI-generated images, or posted by AI accounts, are marked with an AI label so it's always clear what content is AI-made.
Can I delete my own Pulse?
Yes — open the ⋯ menu on your own Pulse and choose Delete. This removes it for everyone permanently.

Likes, Replies & Reposts

Engaging with other people's Pulses.

How do I like a Pulse?
Tap the heart icon under any Pulse. The author is notified, and the Pulse is saved to your Likes so you can find it again later.
How do I reply or comment?
Tap the reply icon under a Pulse to open the reply composer. Replies appear in a thread under the original Pulse, and the author gets notified.
What's the difference between Repost and Quote?
Repost shares the Pulse as-is to your followers. Quote lets you add your own comment on top while linking back to the original Pulse. Both are available from the repost icon.
How do I bookmark a Pulse?
Tap the bookmark icon to save a Pulse privately. Only you can see your bookmarks — find them anytime under Bookmarks in the sidebar.
How do mentions and hashtags work?
Typing @handle tags that account and sends a notification — tagging @nexor triggers an automatic AI reply. Adding #hashtags makes your Pulse discoverable under that topic and shapes your personalized "For You" feed.
Can I view all images in a Pulse full-screen?
Yes — tap any image to open the lightbox viewer. Swipe or use the arrow keys to move between multiple images, and tap outside or press Esc to close.

Profile & Following

Your profile, follow requests, and editing details.

How do I edit my profile?
Go to your profile and tap Edit profile to update your name, bio, avatar, and other details.
How do I follow or unfollow someone?
Tap Follow on any profile to see their Pulses in your feed. If their account is private, your request needs to be approved first. Tap the button again to unfollow.
Where do I see my followers and following?
Tap the follower/following counts on any profile to open the list. From your own profile this also shows pending follow requests if your account is private.
What is the verified badge and how do I get it?
The verified badge appears next to verified accounts' names. Apply from Settings → Get verified — once approved you also get a higher 780-character post limit.
What's the difference between the verified tick and the org badge?
The org badge appears alongside the verified tick (never replacing it) for official organization accounts like @nexor.
How do I see "About this account" info?
Tap the verified badge (or the ⋯ menu) on a profile to see details like join date and other account info.

Discover & Feed

Explore, search, and how your feed is built.

What's the difference between "For You" and "Following"?
Following shows Pulses only from accounts you follow, in recency order. For You is personalized — built from the hashtags in Pulses you've liked, reposted, bookmarked, or written, ranked alongside recency and engagement, all computed on your device.
How do I search for people, hashtags, or Pulses?
Use the search bar in Explore to look up people, hashtags, or Pulses across the app.
How do hashtag pages work?
Tapping any #hashtag opens a dedicated page showing every public Pulse that used it.

Notifications

Staying on top of activity on your account.

What triggers a notification?
You'll be notified for likes, replies, reposts, new followers, and mentions. Check them anytime under Alerts in the sidebar.
What does the number badge on Alerts mean?
It shows your count of unread notifications — it clears once you open the Alerts tab.

Lists & Analytics

Organizing accounts and tracking your own Pulses.

What are Lists for?
Lists let you group accounts into a custom timeline — for example "News" or "Friends" — so you can view their Pulses together. Create one from the Lists tab.
Public vs. private lists — what's the difference?
Public lists can be viewed by anyone. Private lists are visible only to you. Choose this when creating or editing a list.
What does Analytics show me?
Analytics gives you a breakdown of how your own Pulses are performing — engagement like likes, replies, and reposts over time.

Privacy, Safety & Settings

Controlling who sees your content and who can reach you.

How do I make my account private?
Go to Settings → Private account and toggle it on. Once private, only approved followers can see your Pulses, and new followers must be approved.
How do I block or mute someone?
Open the ⋯ menu on their profile and choose Block or Mute. Blocking also removes any existing follow between you. Manage your full lists under Settings → Blocked accounts / Muted accounts.
How do I report a Pulse or account?
Open the ⋯ menu on the Pulse or profile and choose Report, then select a reason. Reports are reviewed and kept confidential.
How do I turn sensitive-content blur on or off?
In Settings, toggle Blur sensitive images — this controls whether adult-flagged images are automatically blurred with a warning overlay.
Can I show or hide my location on my profile?
Yes — toggle Settings → Location sharing to control whether your "Account based in" country shows on your About info.
Where do I set my date of birth?
Go to Settings → Date of birth to set or update it.
Will Sparks share my email, password, or personal data?
No — private account information such as emails, passwords, and personal data is never shared, including by the @nexor AI assistant.

@nexor AI Assistant

Sparks' built-in help account.

Who is @nexor?
@nexor is Sparks' official AI account — it answers questions about how the app works whenever you tag it, running right in your browser with no outside servers involved.
How do I get a reply from @nexor?
Mention @nexor in a Pulse or reply with your question — it'll respond automatically with a relevant answer about Sparks features.
