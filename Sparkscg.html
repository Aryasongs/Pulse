Community Guidelines

Welcome to Nexor Sparks. Our mission is to give everyone the power to create and share ideas instantly, without barriers. To ensure a safe, authentic, and engaging environment, we ask all users to strictly follow these global rules.

1. Core Principles & Safety

Safety is our top priority. We believe that people should feel safe to express themselves freely. However, freedom of expression does not mean freedom to harm others. We do not tolerate behavior that threatens, promotes violence, or endangers anyone's physical or mental well-being.

Violence and Incitement

You may not threaten violence against an individual or a group of people. We also prohibit the glorification of violence. This includes praising acts of terrorism, mass shootings, or any other violent crimes. Sparks will immediately suspend any account found promoting extremist groups or violent acts.

Self-Harm & Suicide

We care deeply about our community. You may not promote, encourage, or provide instructions on self-harm or suicide. If you are sharing personal experiences with self-harm for the purpose of seeking help or spreading awareness, we allow it, but we may apply a sensitive content warning to protect other users.

Child Safety & Exploitation

We have a zero-tolerance policy against child sexual exploitation. Any media depicting the sexual exploitation of minors will be immediately removed, the account permanently banned, and the details forwarded to the relevant international law enforcement agencies.

Zero Tolerance Rule: Accounts participating in child exploitation, terrorism promotion, or extreme real-world violence coordination will face an immediate, unappealable permanent ban and IP restriction.

2. Sensitive Content & NSFW Rules

Sparks is designed to be a platform for diverse content, but we must balance this with the comfort of users who do not wish to see adult or highly graphic material. We have implemented robust AI-driven systems to manage this.

Adult Content (NSFW)

You may share consensually produced adult content, provided it is properly marked. Sparks employs an on-device TensorFlow AI engine that automatically detects and blurs adult imagery. Bypassing this filter intentionally or using adult media in your Profile Avatar or Cover Banner is strictly prohibited.

Graphic Violence

You may not share excessively gory or violent media for the purpose of shocking or disturbing users. News-related, documentary, or educational media showing violence must be kept behind the sensitive content blur.

3. Harassment & Hateful Conduct

We want Sparks to be a place where people can engage in healthy debate, but we draw a hard line at abuse. You may not engage in targeted harassment or coordinate others to attack individuals.

Hateful Speech

You may not promote violence against, threaten, or harass other people on the basis of race, ethnicity, national origin, caste, sexual orientation, gender, gender identity, religious affiliation, age, disability, or serious disease. Use of slurs intended to degrade or intimidate is prohibited.

Doxxing and Privacy

You may not publish or post other people's private information without their express authorization and permission. This includes sharing personal home addresses, non-public phone numbers, financial information, or private identity documents.

4. AI & Synthetic Media (Sparks AI)

Nexor Sparks integrates advanced AI capabilities, including our official @nexor bot for text and image generation. While we encourage creativity, AI must be used responsibly.

Misleading Synthetic Media (Deepfakes)

You may not deceptively share synthetic or manipulated media that is likely to cause harm. This includes deepfakes designed to manipulate elections, create non-consensual sexual content, or falsely depict public figures committing crimes.

Sparks AI Usage Rules

  • Prompt Safety: Attempting to bypass the @nexor AI safety filters to generate prohibited content (violence, nudity, exploitation) will result in a strike against your account.
  • AI Watermarks: Images generated via @nexor carry a permanent embedded watermark and a UI "AI" label. Attempting to crop out or remove this watermark to pass the image off as a real photograph is a violation of these guidelines.
  • AI Accounts: If you are operating an account fully automated by a bot or AI script, you must explicitly check the "This is an AI account" option in your Profile Settings so users are aware.

5. Authenticity, Spam, and Platform Manipulation

The value of the Sparks network relies on authentic interactions. We prohibit behaviors that artificially inflate engagement or manipulate the "For You" algorithm.

Spam & Automation

You may not use Sparks' services for the purpose of spamming anyone. This includes:

  • Selling, purchasing, or attempting to artificially inflate followers, likes, or reposts.
  • Using automated scripts to aggressively follow/unfollow users.
  • Posting identical, repetitive Pulses across multiple accounts or constantly replying with the same generic links.

Impersonation & Fake Accounts

You may not impersonate individuals, groups, or organizations to mislead, confuse, or deceive others. Parody, commentary, and fan accounts are allowed but must clearly indicate in their Display Name and Bio that they are not affiliated with the actual entity.

6. Verification & Organization Badges

Sparks offers a Blue Tick verification system and a Gold Organization Badge system to establish authenticity for notable figures and companies.

Providing false information, forged identity documents, or attempting to bribe staff to obtain a verified badge will result in the permanent suspension of your account. If a verified account changes its handle or fundamentally alters its identity to deceive users, the badge will be instantly revoked without warning.

7. Enforcement, Strikes, and Appeals

When we determine that a user has violated these Community Guidelines, we take a tiered approach to enforcement, depending on the severity of the violation.

The Strike System:
  • First Strike: A warning and the removal of the violating Pulse.
  • Second Strike: A temporary read-only mode for 7 days (you can browse, but cannot post, like, or reply).
  • Third Strike: Permanent account suspension.

Appealing a Decision

If you believe your account was suspended or your Pulse was removed by mistake, you can submit an appeal. Appeals are reviewed by human moderators. However, accounts banned for extreme violations (like child exploitation or terrorism) are not eligible for appeals.

8. Final Legal Agreement

By registering and using the Nexor Sparks platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to be bound by these Community Guidelines. We reserve the right to modify these guidelines at any time without prior individual notice. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated rules.
