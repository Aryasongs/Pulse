📌 How It Works

  1. Register your dog with details, photo, and vaccination history
  2. Get a unique LMDC-ID and official digital health card with QR code
  3. Scan QR from any device to instantly view the dog's full medical record
  4. Update vaccination records anytime by submitting proof documents
  5. Apply for medical funds by providing diagnosis reports and bills
