Project Milestone & Vision

Why Pulse Was Created?

Pulse Music was conceptualized as a highly scalable premium solution to disrupt standard audio streaming boundaries. The main vision was to build an ultra-fast hybrid architecture capable of merging complex dynamic cloud authentication layers with local iOS hardware UI configurations smoothly.

Project Launch (2024)

The conceptual design, secure database blueprints, and real-time audio pipeline research protocols were officially launched in late 2024.

Final Testing Phase (2026)
