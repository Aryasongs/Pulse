1. Introduction and Scope

This Global Privacy Policy explains how Nexor Sparks ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects the personal data of our users ("you," "your"). This document serves as our binding privacy agreement, universally applicable regardless of the geographic location from which you access our platform.

By creating an account, posting a "Pulse," or otherwise interacting with our platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood the terms established in this legal framework. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect the information absolutely necessary to provide, secure, and enhance our microblogging platform. The categories of Personal Information we collect include:

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Credentials: Email address, cryptographically hashed passwords, and Date of Birth (DOB) collected during the registration process to ensure compliance with minimum age laws[span_1](start_span)[span_1](end_span).
  • Profile Identifiers: Your unique `@handle`, Display Name, biographical text, website URL, location, pronouns, and languages spoken[span_2](start_span)[span_2](end_span).
  • User-Generated Content (UGC): The text, base64 encoded images, and compressed `.webm` video files you upload in your "Pulses," alongside your replies, quotes, and direct communications[span_3](start_span)[span_3](end_span).
  • Verification Data: If you apply for a Verified Blue Tick or an Organization Badge, we collect your full legal name, statements of intent, and provided external identity links to process your application[span_4](start_span)[span_4](end_span).

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Engagement Metrics: We log mathematical interactions, including likes, reposts, bookmarks, and thread replies, to compute engagement scores[span_5](start_span)[span_5](end_span).
  • Technical Device Data: Generalized IP addresses (used strictly during signup for initial localization mapping), browser types, and timestamp metadata associated with account creation and session authentication[span_6](start_span)[span_6](end_span).
  • Behavioral Interest Data: Localized extraction of `#hashtags` from your engagement history to construct an on-device "Interest Profile" for ranking your "For You" feed[span_7](start_span)[span_7](end_span).

3. How We Process Your Data

Your data is processed strictly for the operational functionality and enhancement of Nexor Sparks. We utilize your data to:

  • Facilitate the creation, moderation, and distribution of your Pulses across the network.
  • Dynamically generate and deliver the "For You" and "Following" feeds utilizing client-side algorithmic sorting[span_8](start_span)[span_8](end_span).
  • Deliver real-time push notifications and alerts regarding mentions, replies, and follow requests[span_9](start_span)[span_9](end_span).
  • Provide you with statistical insights via the "Creator Analytics" dashboard[span_10](start_span)[span_10](end_span).
  • Detect, prevent, and mitigate spam, fraud, terms-of-service violations, and security vulnerabilities.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

For users residing in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom (UK), we process your Personal Data under the following lawful bases defined by Article 6 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing required to provide the core services governed by our Terms of Service (e.g., creating your account and publishing your posts).
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary for maintaining platform security, detecting fraud, and developing new features, provided these interests are not overridden by your fundamental rights.
  • Consent: Where required by law, we rely on your explicit consent, such as for accessing specific device permissions or processing special categories of data.
  • Legal Obligation: Processing required to comply with binding law enforcement requests or judicial orders.

5. Artificial Intelligence & Local Processing

Nexor Sparks is built with cutting-edge AI architecture. We have structured our AI features to prioritize user privacy fundamentally.

Zero-Upload On-Device NSFW Scanning
To enforce safety without compromising privacy, our Sensitive Content filter utilizes a TensorFlow.js model (`nsfwjs`) that downloads directly to your browser. Your images are classified entirely locally on your device's hardware[span_11](start_span)[span_11](end_span). Unposted images are never transmitted to our backend servers for analysis.

Sparks AI (@nexor) Generation

When you explicitly invoke our AI bot by tagging @nexor with a text or image command, the specific text prompt is transmitted via an API to Cloudflare Workers AI[span_12](start_span)[span_12](end_span).

  • No Foundation Model Training: The prompts and generated outputs are used exclusively to fulfill your immediate request. They are strictly prohibited from being utilized to train global foundational language or image models.
  • Automated Transparency: All AI-generated media is permanently stamped with a visible UI badge and an embedded pixel watermark to ensure algorithmic transparency across the ecosystem[span_13](start_span)[span_13](end_span).

6. Data Sharing and Disclosures

We absolutely do not sell your Personal Data to third-party data brokers, marketing agencies, or advertisers. We share your data only in the following restricted scenarios:

  • Public Visibility: Unless your account is set to "Private," your Display Name, Handle, Bio, and Pulses are publicly accessible on the internet[span_14](start_span)[span_14](end_span).
  • Service Providers (Data Processors): We utilize Google Firebase for secure database hosting (`Realtime Database`) and user authentication (`Firebase Auth`)[span_15](start_span)[span_15](end_span). These enterprise partners are bound by strict Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) and cannot utilize your data for their own independent purposes.
  • Legal and Regulatory Authorities: We will disclose data if we determine that such disclosure is legally mandatory to comply with a valid subpoena, court order, or search warrant, or to prevent imminent physical harm or address child sexual exploitation material (CSEM).

7. International Data Transfers

Nexor Sparks operates globally. Consequently, your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries outside of your residential jurisdiction (including the United States), where data protection laws may differ.

When we transfer personal data originating from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland to countries lacking an adequacy decision, we rely on legally validated transfer mechanisms, primarily Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, ensuring your data rights remain fully protected across borders.

8. Your Global Privacy Rights

Regardless of where you live, Nexor Sparks grants you universal control over your digital footprint. You possess the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a comprehensive export of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can instantly update your profile information, bio, and credentials through the in-app Settings menu[span_16](start_span)[span_16](end_span).
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You may delete individual Pulses or permanently terminate your entire account. Deletion removes the data from our active production database[span_17](start_span)[span_17](end_span).
  • Right to Restriction & Objection: You have the ability to toggle your account to "Private," restricting who can process your content[span_18](start_span)[span_18](end_span). You may also disable the auto-blur NSFW feature[span_19](start_span)[span_19](end_span) or hide your localized signup location from your public profile[span_20](start_span)[span_20](end_span).

9. U.S. State-Specific Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a resident of California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, or Utah, state laws provide specific protections regarding your data.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information
Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), you have the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your data for cross-context behavioral advertising. Nexor Sparks does not sell or share your data for these purposes.

We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights. You will not face denial of services, varied pricing, or diminished service quality for utilizing these rights.

10. Data Retention & Deletion

We adhere to strict data retention schedules:

  • Active Accounts: We retain your Profile data, Pulses, and interaction logs for as long as your account remains active, facilitating uninterrupted service delivery.
  • Deleted Content: When you manually delete a Pulse, it is instantly removed from the primary database index and the public feed[span_21](start_span)[span_21](end_span).
  • Account Termination: Upon account deletion, personal identifiers are wiped from the active database. Residual operational logs may remain in encrypted cold-storage backups for a maximum of 30 days before being purged permanently, barring any overriding legal hold.

11. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The Nexor Sparks platform is exclusively designated for users who are at least 13 years of age (or higher if dictated by local digital consent laws in the EU). We strictly mandate Date of Birth verification during the signup workflow[span_22](start_span)[span_22](end_span).

We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or maintain data from anyone under the age of 13. In the event we identify an underage account, we execute immediate administrative termination and permanent erasure of the associated data payload.

12. Security Measures

We deploy robust, multi-layered security architectures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data. This includes:

  • Transport Layer Security (TLS/HTTPS): Encrypting all data transmitted between your device and our endpoints.
  • Database Security: Utilizing Google Firebase's enterprise-grade security rules to restrict read/write access strictly to authenticated, authorized user contexts[span_23](start_span)[span_23](end_span).
  • Media Compression: Automatic client-side canvas compression for images and video transcoding, ensuring minimal payload footprints and reducing interception vectors[span_24](start_span)[span_24](end_span).
