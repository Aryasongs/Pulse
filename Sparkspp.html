Privacy Policy

Your privacy and digital security are fundamental to Nexor Sparks. This document explains exactly what data we collect, how it is processed, and how you maintain complete control over your digital footprint on our platform.

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a seamless and personalized experience, Nexor Sparks collects specific data when you register, use our services, or interact with our community.

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Details: When you create an account, we collect your chosen Display Name, unique @handle, email address, password, and Date of Birth (to verify you meet the minimum age requirement of 13).
  • Profile Information: Any additional information you choose to add to your profile, including your Bio, Profile Photo (Avatar), Cover Banner, Location, Website URL, Pronouns, and Languages.
  • Your Content: The text, images, and videos you publish in your "Pulses" (posts), as well as your replies, likes, bookmarks, and reposts.
  • Direct Communications: Information provided when you submit support tickets or verification requests.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We track how you interact with the platform, such as which Pulses you engage with, accounts you follow, and hashtags you search for. This powers your personalized "For You" feed.
  • Device and Network Information: We may collect basic information regarding your browser type, device operating system, and a generalized IP address location to enhance account security and provide localized experiences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect solely to operate, improve, and secure the Nexor Sparks platform. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertising brokers.

  • Service Delivery: To create your account, publish your Pulses, and deliver notifications.
  • Content Personalization: Your likes, reposts, and bookmarks are analyzed locally by our client-side algorithm to build an interest profile, determining which posts appear in your "For You" feed.
  • Safety and Moderation: To detect spam, investigate reported content, and enforce our Community Guidelines.
  • Analytics: To provide you with your personal Creator Analytics dashboard and to help us understand overall platform usage trends.

3. Artificial Intelligence & Data Processing

Nexor Sparks heavily utilizes Artificial Intelligence to enhance user safety and creativity. We are fully transparent about how your data interacts with these AI models.

On-Device Content Moderation (NSFW Filter)

Sparks uses TensorFlow.js to automatically detect sensitive/adult imagery. This process happens entirely locally on your device (within your browser). Unposted images are never sent to our servers for scanning, ensuring absolute privacy for your personal files.

Sparks AI (@nexor) Generation

When you tag the official @nexor bot to ask a question or generate an image (using the `image` or `text` commands), the text prompt you provide is securely sent to our backend Cloudflare AI Workers to generate the response or artwork. These prompts are processed securely and are not used to train public foundational AI models.

AI Transparency: Any image generated by Sparks AI will always be visibly labeled with an "AI" badge in the UI and a permanent "Nexor AI" watermark on the image pixels.

4. How We Share Your Information

We restrict the sharing of your personal data. We only share information in the following specific circumstances:

  • With the Public: Your public profile information, public Pulses, likes, and replies are visible to everyone on the platform (unless your account is set to Private).
  • With Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for secure backend database hosting and authentication. Your data is encrypted and stored safely within these enterprise-grade cloud environments.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may preserve or disclose your information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to comply with a law, regulation, valid legal process, or governmental request; or to protect the safety of any person (e.g., in cases of child exploitation or physical threats).

5. Your Privacy Controls & Rights

You own your data. Nexor Sparks provides you with comprehensive tools to control your digital footprint.

  • Account Privacy: You can toggle your account to "Private" in Settings. Once private, only users you explicitly approve can view your Pulses.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete any Pulse, reply, or media file at any time. Once deleted, it is permanently removed from our active databases.
  • Blocking and Muting: You have full control over who you interact with. You can block or mute any user, which severs their ability to communicate with you or appear in your feeds.
  • Location Hiding: If you do not want your generalized country/location visible on your "About this account" page, you can hide it via the Settings menu.

6. Data Security & Retention

We use industry-standard encryption protocols (including HTTPS/SSL) to protect your data in transit, and secure cloud environments to protect it at rest. We retain your profile information and Pulses for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your data will be queued for permanent deletion from our production servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Nexor Sparks is not directed at children. You must be at least 13 years old to use our services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete the account and the associated data.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal obligations. The most current version will always be available on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect the latest revision. By continuing to access or use Sparks after those changes become effective, you agree to be bound by the revised Privacy Policy.
